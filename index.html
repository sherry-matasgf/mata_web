What is MATA?

MATA is short for the Mid-America Technology Alliance. We are a group of individuals, entrepreneurs, and investors whose primary goal is to attract, cultivate and promote technology talent and businesses throughout the region.

What does MATA do?

We support a culture of innovation and entrpreneurship by hosting and promoting events that are important to our tech community. Our 2nd Thursday events feature five local entrepreneurs or disruptive thinkers who speak for five minutes about their businesses or ideas they have to make Springfield a better place to live and work. We are also working on hosting a hack-a-thon this fall. Keep checking our web site for more activities!

Who should join?

Our organization is open to any individual, organization, or business passionate about technology and with an interest in cultivating an entrepreneurial spirit in southwest Missouri.
